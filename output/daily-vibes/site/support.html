Common questions

How do I turn on daily notifications?

Open the app and go to Settings. Toggle on Daily Notification and choose your preferred time. You'll receive one motivational quote per day at the time you choose. You can change or disable this at any time.

Can I choose which quotes appear?

You can browse all 460+ quotes by category inside the app and tap the heart to save your favourites. Notifications pull from the full curated library. A dedicated favourites-only mode is something we're considering for a future update.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. All 460+ quotes are stored on your device. Daily Vibes For You requires no internet connection at any time — not for setup, not for quotes, not for anything.

What devices are supported?

Daily Vibes For You runs on iPhone (iOS 15.1+), iPad (iPadOS 15.1+), Mac (macOS 12.0+), and Apple Vision Pro (visionOS 1.0+). It is not available on Android.

Is the app free? Are there in-app purchases?

The app is completely free. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or ads — now or ever.

Does the app collect any data?

No. Daily Vibes For You collects zero personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no servers. Everything stays on your device. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

I found a bug — how do I report it?

Please contact Ananya Banerjee directly using the contact information in the Privacy Policy. Include your iOS version, device model, and a description of what happened. Bug reports are genuinely appreciated and read personally.
